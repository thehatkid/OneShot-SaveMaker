Notice

This generates a Regular or Post-Solstice, Beated or Not save file for OneShot, for those who lost their save but want to play OneShot or to mod OneShot: Fading Memory without replaying the base game.

Do NOT use if You haven't beaten Solstice yet, since You'd be missing a ton of context.

If You want full save, You need fill Your In-Game Name and check all checkboxes.

Also, You may consider replaying the base game with OneShot: Fading Memory installed anyway, since there are subtle easter eggs you might find.
